Rules of Behavior

The following behaviors will not be tolerated and will result in the Flagging of a User’s account. A Flag suspends the user, halting engagement on the Idea Board until the Administrator removes your restrictions.

  • Use of profanity
  • Threats, personal or professional
  • Airing of personal grievances against colleagues
  • Sharing of Personally Identifiable Information such as a social security number, health records, etc.
  • Personal attacks or comments on an individual’s performance, personal life, etc.
  • Off-topic comments or questions on an Idea thread
  • Derogatory, ugly and otherwise unproductive comments
